Frequently Asked Questions

What is trotro.live?

A digital form of your regular trotro transit. According to Wikipedia, in Ghana and neighboring countries trotros are private owned mini-buses or shared taxis that travel fixed routes leaving when filled to capacity. While there are trotro stations, these vehicles can also be boarded anywhere along the route.

Why Trotro live?

It helps citizens, tourists, passengers and etc

  1. To know your trotro fare from point A to point B.
  2. To report and track missing items on trotro vehicles within 6 hours.
  3. In June 2021, we will launch a microchip (still in production) which will aid us to track, book trotro transit, a plus for trotro car owners.

How do you benefit from Trotro Live?

  1. Regular users can contribute our database, by giving us accurate trotro fare and earn pesewas in exchange, with aid of web monetization.
  2. Drivers and trotro mates can find each other, hire, search for co-workers and even meet trotro car owners directly.
  3. In our September, 2021 feature tourists, visitors will easily navigate from hotels, airports, etc to trotro stations and knowing the stories behind the town and tell the culture.
  4. Thoughts, concerns, new policies and motions can be posted on trotro digital to help regulate trotro.live, transport for the people, by the people.

How much do you earn by contributing data?

Users can earn upto 100ghc in exchange of 1000 lines of accurate trotro fares on different routes and other merchandise.
